Green Smoothie Recipe

A super charged, super healthy, nutrient-dense green dream!

Smooth and mildy sweet, this recipe will give your body energy and bring your mind focus.

This smoothie will help you transform your body and mind in powerful ways. Blending super nutrient-dense greens like spinach and kale is a great way to get your body the nutrition it craves while boosting your immune system

Most store bought smoothies are made with fruit juices and added sugar. This smoothie uses raw ingredients and coconut water to keep create a nutrional, delicious meal

Jam packed with essential vitamins, this smoothie is quick and easy to make, with only need 4 ingredients needed to make it!

Ingredients

  • Spinach
  • Kale
  • Coconut Water
  • Frozen Pineapple

Steps

  1. Chop (1) cup of spinach and (1) cup of kale and add to blender
  2. Add (1) cup of coconut water to the blender and blend until smooth
  3. Add (1) cup of frozen pineapple chunks and blend together until smooth
  4. Pour into a cup and add ice
  5. Enjoy!
