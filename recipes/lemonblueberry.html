Lemon Blueberry Smoothie Recipe

Sweet, tangy and healthy. This citrus smoothie is a great afternoon pick-me-up.

Bright flavor and fresh, juicy taste. This easy, vegan smoothie is naturally sweetened with fruit and made with only five healthy ingredients

Dairy-free, vegan, and great for adding your own additional ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Blueberryies
  • Frozen Bananas
  • Lemon
  • Almond Extract
  • Almond Milk

Steps

  1. Chop 1 frozen banana into chunks
  2. Add banana, 1/2 cup blueberries, 1 cup almond milk, and 1 TSP of Almond Extract to blender
  3. Squeeze 1/2 fresh lemon juice into blender
  4. blend together until smooth
  5. Add ice and enjoy!
